## Who we are

Above is a senior team of engineers and advisors who have spent our careers managing Mac fleets, security infrastructure, and IT strategy for organizations of all sizes.

We built the company on a simple belief: teams that run best on Apple should not have to settle for IT support that treats macOS and iOS as an afterthought. We treat the Apple ecosystem as our foundation, ensuring your technology feels like a tool rather than a hurdle.

## What makes us different

Most managed service providers are built for Windows-first environments, with Apple support added as an extra layer. Above is built the other way around.

By centering our practice on Apple Business Manager, MDM, and the specific nuances of your workflow, we provide faster troubleshooting and a smoother experience for your team. We don't offer workarounds; we offer native solutions.

## Who we work with

We partner with growing companies that rely primarily on Mac and iOS to get their work done.

Our ideal clients are teams of 10 to 500 employees who want a long-term partner rather than a transactional vendor. We specialize in filling the gap for companies that don't have a dedicated IT leader, providing both day-to-day support and the fractional advisory needed to scale your infrastructure safely.

If that sounds like your team, [get in touch](/#contact). We would love to talk.
